Fried chicken

Description

To make fried chicken, coat the chicken pieces in seasoned flour until fully covered. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the chicken until golden, crispy, and cooked through. Let it rest briefly, then serve hot with your favorite dip or side.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken drumsticks or thighs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika (optional)
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, pepper, and paprika in a bowl
  2. Coat chicken evenly in the flour mixture
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan
  4. Fry chicken for 12â€“15 minutes, turning occasionally
  5. Remove when golden and cooked through
