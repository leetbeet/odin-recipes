Pizza

Description

To make a simple pizza, start by spreading tomato sauce over a rolled-out pizza dough. Add shredded mozzarella cheese, then top with sliced vegetables, pepperoni, or any toppings you like. Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C (425°F) for about 12â€“15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted. Slice and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shreded mozarella cheese
  • Toppings such as pepperoni, onions or bell peppers

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F)
  2. Roll out the dough on a baking tray
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly
  4. Sprinkle cheese and add toppings
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden
  6. Slice and serve hot
