Fries

Description

To make crispy fries, peel and cut potatoes into thin strips. Rinse and dry them well, then deep fry in hot oil until golden and crunchy. Sprinkle with salt while hot and serve fresh as a snack or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips
  2. Rinse and pat them dry
  3. Heat oil in a pan
  4. Fry potatoes in batches until golden
  5. Drain and sprinkle with salt
